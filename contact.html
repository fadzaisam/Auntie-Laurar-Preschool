Frequently Asked Questions

What are the preschool's hours of operation?

Our preschool operates from 7:00 am to 6:00 pm, Monday through Friday.

What is the age range for children at the preschool?

We accept children from 2 to 5 years old.

What is the preschool's educational philosophy?

Our preschool follows a child-centered approach, focusing on play-based learning and hands-on activities.

What are the qualifications of the preschool teachers?

All our teachers hold a degree in Early Childhood Education and have extensive experience working with young children.

How many children are in each class?

Our class sizes are small, with a maximum of 12 children per class.

<

Can I visit the preschool to observe a class?

Yes, we welcome parents to visit and observe a class. Please schedule an appointment with our administrator.

How does the preschool communicate with parents?

We use a variety of methods, including daily reports, weekly newsletters, and parent-teacher conferences.

What is the preschool's tuition and payment policy?

Our tuition rates are competitive, and we offer flexible payment plans. Please contact our administrator for more information.
